# 🚀 Rocky Command Centre (RCC) - PWA Edition

Your personal dashboard, now installable as an app!

## What's New?

✅ **PWA Support** - Install RCC as an app on any device!  
✅ **Offline Access** - Works even without internet (once cached)  
✅ **App Icon** - Looks like a real app on your home screen  
✅ **Fast Loading** - Cached for instant access  

## Files in This Folder

- `index.html` - Your updated dashboard (with PWA features!)
- `data.json` - Your calendar, tasks, contacts, and notes
- `manifest.json` - Tells devices "I'm an app!"
- `service-worker.js` - Makes offline mode work
- `create-icons.html` - Tool to generate app icons
- `icon-192.png` - Small app icon (you'll create this)
- `icon-512.png` - Large app icon (you'll create this)

## How to Install RCC as an App

### On iPad/iPhone:
1. Open Safari and go to your RCC URL
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. Done! RCC is now on your home screen! 🎉

### On Android:
1. Open Chrome and go to your RCC URL
2. Tap the **menu** (three dots)
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Tap **"Install"**
5. Done! 🎉

### On Computer (Chrome/Edge):
1. Open your RCC URL in Chrome or Edge
2. Look for the **install button** in the address bar (looks like a + or download icon)
3. Click it and choose **"Install"**
4. RCC opens in its own window like a real app! 🎉

## Creating Your App Icons

Right now, you need to create the icon files:

1. **Open `create-icons.html` in Safari** (double-click it)
2. **Click the download links** to save both icons
3. **Rename them:**
   - One to `icon-192.png`
   - One to `icon-512.png`
4. **Put them in the RCC folder** (same place as index.html)

OR if you want custom icons, you can:
- Design your own in any graphics app
- Ask Rocky to generate a cooler design
- Use any image you like (just resize to 192x192 and 512x512)

## How to Access RCC

Just tap the RCC icon on your home screen! 📱
